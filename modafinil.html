Legal & Medical Information

FDA Approved Uses:

  • Narcolepsy
  • Shift work sleep disorder
  • Excessive daytime sleepiness associated with obstructive sleep apnea

Off-Label Use: Some physicians may prescribe modafinil off-label for cognitive enhancement, but this requires medical evaluation and supervision.

CRITICAL SAFETY INFORMATION

This medication requires medical supervision and is not available without prescription.

  • NEVER obtain modafinil from unregulated sources
  • Can cause serious side effects including skin reactions, psychiatric symptoms
