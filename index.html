How to Play:

Watch the grid for position and listen for spoken letters.

Press A if the current position matches the position from N steps back.

Press Z if the current letter matches the letter from N steps back.

You can respond to either one or both modalities - no penalty for not responding.

You have 2.5 seconds to respond once the N-back threshold is reached.
