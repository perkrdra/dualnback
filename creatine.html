Scientific Research

Memory Enhancement: A 2018 systematic review found that creatine supplementation improved short-term memory and intelligence/reasoning in healthy individuals.
Cognitive Processing: Research published in Psychopharmacology showed that vegetarians taking creatine performed better on memory and intelligence tests.
Mental Fatigue: Studies demonstrate that creatine supplementation can reduce mental fatigue and improve performance on cognitive tasks requiring sustained attention.

Recommended Dosage

Loading Phase (Optional): 20g per day (split into 4 doses) for 5-7 days

Maintenance Dose: 3-5g per day

Best Time: Can be taken any time of day, with or without food

Form: Creatine monohydrate is the most researched and cost-effective form

Important Safety Information

While creatine is generally considered safe for healthy adults, you should:

  • Consult with a healthcare provider before starting supplementation
  • Stay well-hydrated when taking creatine
  • Be aware that initial weight gain (1-3 lbs) from water retention is normal
  • Avoid if you have kidney disease or are at risk for kidney problems
