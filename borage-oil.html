Recommended Dosage

Standard Dose: 500-1000mg per day (providing 100-240mg of GLA)

Timing: Take with meals to improve absorption and reduce potential stomach upset

Quality: Look for cold-pressed, organic borage oil for highest purity

Storage: Keep refrigerated and away from light to prevent oxidation

Important Safety Information

Borage oil is generally safe but consider the following:

  • Consult with a healthcare provider before starting supplementation
  • May interact with blood-thinning medications
  • Can lower seizure threshold - avoid if you have epilepsy
  • May cause mild digestive upset in some individuals
  • Pregnant and breastfeeding women should consult their doctor
  • Choose products tested for pyrrolizidine alkaloids (PAs)
  • Start with lower doses to assess tolerance
