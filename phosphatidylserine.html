Scientific Research

Memory Performance: Clinical trials show that 100mg of PS daily for 12 weeks significantly improved memory performance in healthy adults.
Cognitive Decline: Research published in Neurology found that PS supplementation may slow the progression of cognitive decline in elderly populations.
Stress & Cortisol: Studies demonstrate that PS supplementation can reduce exercise-induced stress and lower cortisol levels, supporting cognitive function under stress.

Recommended Dosage

Standard Dose: 100-300mg per day

Best Form: Soy-derived or sunflower-derived PS (avoid bovine-derived)

Timing: Take with meals for better absorption

Duration: Benefits typically seen after 6-12 weeks of consistent use

Important Safety Information

PS is generally well-tolerated, but consider the following:

  • Consult with a healthcare provider before starting supplementation
  • May interact with blood-thinning medications
  • Some people may experience mild insomnia if taken late in the day
  • Choose plant-derived sources to avoid potential contamination risks
