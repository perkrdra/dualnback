Recommended Dosage

Starting Dose: 5-10g (1-2 teaspoons) per day

Optimal Dose: 10-15g per day for cognitive benefits

Timing: Take on an empty stomach or with coffee for faster absorption

Important Safety Information

C8 is generally safe but may cause digestive issues if not introduced properly:

  • Start with very small doses (1-2g) to assess tolerance
  • May cause digestive upset, nausea, or diarrhea if taken in large amounts initially
  • Take with food if experiencing stomach discomfort
  • Gradually increase dosage over 1-2 weeks
  • Consult healthcare provider if you have digestive conditions
  • May interact with certain medications - consult your doctor
