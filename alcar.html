Recommended Dosage

Standard Dose: 500-2000mg per day

Timing: Take on an empty stomach for best absorption, preferably in the morning

Split Doses: Divide into 2-3 doses throughout the day for sustained benefits

Start Low: Begin with 500mg and gradually increase based on response

Important Safety Information

ALCAR is generally well-tolerated, but consider the following:

  • Consult with a healthcare provider before starting supplementation
  • May cause mild restlessness or insomnia if taken late in the day
  • Some people may experience mild nausea on an empty stomach
  • May have a fishy odor - this is normal and indicates freshness
  • Can interact with thyroid medications - consult your doctor
  • Start with lower doses to assess individual tolerance
