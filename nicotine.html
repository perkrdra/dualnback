Scientific Research

Working Memory Enhancement: 2024 studies confirm nicotine enhances working memory, attention, and executive function through nicotinic acetylcholine receptor activation in key brain regions.
Brain Network Effects: Neuroimaging shows nicotine increases activity in executive control regions while reducing default-mode network activity, optimizing cognitive performance.

Cognitive Domains: Meta-analyses show nicotine particularly enhances attention, working memory, fine motor skills, and episodic memory functions.

Important: Never establish a regular pattern of use to avoid addiction
