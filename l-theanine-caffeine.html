When to Take

30-60 minutes before training: Take the combination on an empty stomach for fastest absorption and peak effects during your cognitive training session.

Duration of effects: 2-4 hours, perfect for extended training sessions.

Frequency: Can be used daily, but consider cycling (5 days on, 2 days off) to prevent caffeine tolerance.

Time of day: Best taken in the morning or early afternoon to avoid interference with sleep.
