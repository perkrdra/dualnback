Scientific Research

Memory Performance: Studies show that higher choline intake is associated with better performance on memory tests and reduced age-related memory decline.
Cognitive Development: Research published in the American Journal of Clinical Nutrition found that adequate choline levels support optimal brain development and function.
Attention & Processing: Clinical trials demonstrate that choline supplementation can improve attention span and cognitive processing speed in healthy adults.

Recommended Dosage

Daily Intake: 300-500mg per day

Best Forms: Alpha-GPC (highly bioavailable) or CDP-Choline (Citicoline)

Timing: Take with meals to improve absorption

Food Sources: Eggs, liver, fish, and nuts are natural sources of choline

Important Safety Information

Choline supplementation is generally safe, but you should be aware of:

  • Consult with a healthcare provider before starting supplementation
  • High doses (>3g/day) may cause fishy body odor or digestive upset
  • Start with lower doses and gradually increase as needed
  • Pregnant and breastfeeding women should consult their doctor before use
